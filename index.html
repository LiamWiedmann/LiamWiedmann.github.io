MIDI Reverb Effect

Liam Wiedmann

This MIDI effect adds reverb to MIDI notes. This website takes a Midi note, and applies reverb to the note. There is a slider to adjust how much reverb there is from 0 to 10 seconds, and a display just below. In addition, the color of the background changes with the slider, with blue being at 0 seconds, and dynamically changing to green at 10 seconds. Most of the work I did was in the formatting section of the html.

Reverb Amount: 1 second
